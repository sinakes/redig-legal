Privacy Policy for Redig

Last Updated: November 27, 2025

Overview

Redig ("we," "our," or "the app") is a fitness tracking application developed by Modum Ventures. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Workout Data

  • Exercise names, sets, reps, and weights
  • Workout dates and duration
  • Training session history
  • Custom exercise configurations
  • Machine settings and notes

Device Information

  • Device type and iOS version (for app compatibility)
  • Anonymous crash reports (if enabled)

Purchase Information

  • Subscription status (Premium/Free tier)
  • Purchase receipts (processed by Apple, not stored by us)

How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

  • Provide Core Functionality: Track your workouts and display progress
  • Sync Across Devices: Use iCloud to sync your data between your Apple devices
  • Premium Features: Verify subscription status to unlock premium features
  • Analytics: Show you your workout history, progress charts, and insights

We Do NOT

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Share your workout data with advertisers
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other personal data
  • Create advertising profiles based on your fitness data

Data Storage and Sync

Local Storage

All your workout data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled, your data syncs across your Apple devices via Apple's CloudKit service. This data is:

  • Encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Stored in your personal iCloud account
  • Subject to Apple's privacy policies
  • Not accessible by Redig developers

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device and iCloud account until you delete it. We do not maintain separate copies of your data on our servers.

Premium Subscriptions

Subscription Management

  • Subscriptions are processed entirely through Apple's App Store
  • We receive only confirmation of subscription status, not payment details
  • Apple handles all billing, receipts, and payment information
  • Manage subscriptions through iOS Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions

Subscription Types

  • Monthly Premium ($4.99/month)
  • Yearly Premium ($39.99/year)
  • Lifetime Premium ($149.99 one-time)

Auto-Renewal

  • Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled 24 hours before the end of the current period
  • Your Apple ID account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period
  • You can manage and cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID account settings

Third-Party Services

Apple Services

We use the following Apple services:

  • CloudKit: For iCloud data sync
  • StoreKit: For in-app purchases
  • App Store Connect: For app distribution

These services are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics services. Any analytics shown in the app are calculated locally on your device.

Your Rights

Data Access

You can access all your workout data directly within the app.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data by:

  • Deleting individual workouts within the app
  • Deleting the app (removes local data)
  • Disabling iCloud sync and deleting iCloud data via iOS Settings

Data Portability

We are working on features to export your workout data.

Children's Privacy

Redig is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

Security

We implement security measures including:

  • All data sync uses Apple's encrypted CloudKit
  • No external servers store your personal data
  • Subscription verification uses Apple's secure StoreKit APIs

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or App Store update notes.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy:

  • Email: redig@modumventures.com

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

EU residents have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Since all data is stored locally and in your personal iCloud, you maintain full control over your data.
