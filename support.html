Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a workout program?

Tap "Build a Program" on the Workout tab. You can create multi-split programs (like Push/Pull/Legs) that cycle automatically, or standalone workouts for flexible training.

How do I track a set during my workout?

During a training session, tap on the set row to log your weight and reps. Swipe right or tap the checkmark to complete a set. The app will automatically start your rest timer.

What do the different set types mean?

Redig supports advanced training methods:

  • Warmup (W): Lighter weight to prepare muscles
  • Working Set (1, 2, 3...): Your main training sets
  • Dropset (DS): Reduce weight mid-set without rest
  • Rest-Pause (RP): Brief pause within set to extend reps
  • Cluster (C): Mini-sets with short rests between
  • Superset (SS): Back-to-back with another exercise
  • To Failure (F): Push to muscular failure
  • Deload (D): Reduced intensity for recovery

Is my data backed up?

Yes! If you have iCloud enabled, your workout data automatically syncs across all your Apple devices. Your data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account.

Can I use Redig offline?

Absolutely. All features work offline. Your data syncs automatically when you're back online.

Subscription & Billing

Managing Your Subscription

All subscriptions are managed through Apple. To view, change, or cancel your subscription:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Select Redig to manage

Restore Purchases

If you've previously purchased Premium and it's not showing:

  1. Open Redig
  2. Go to Settings (profile icon)
  3. Tap "Restore Purchases"

Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

Troubleshooting

App is running slowly

  • Make sure you're running the latest version of Redig
  • Try closing and reopening the app
  • Restart your device if issues persist

Data not syncing between devices

  • Ensure iCloud is enabled: Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud
  • Check that Redig has iCloud access enabled
  • Make sure both devices are connected to the internet
  • iCloud sync can take a few minutes for large amounts of data

Workout data seems missing

  • Pull down to refresh on the Workout tab
  • Check if you're looking at the correct program/workout
  • Allow time for iCloud to sync if using multiple devices
